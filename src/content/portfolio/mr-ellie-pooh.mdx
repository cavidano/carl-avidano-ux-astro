## Challenge

When I first learned about Mr. Ellie pooh, I knew I had to work with them. Their fair trade paper products are made from sanitized elephant dung! 

I met with the owner and discovered their need for an e-commerce store that could support retail and wholesale sales. The website also needed to showcase a growing catalog of handmade products while highlighting the conservation efforts and artisan communities behind the organization. 

We also agreed that their existing product photography failed to capture the quality and craftsmanship of the products, making it difficult to communicate their value online.

## Solution

After evaluating several ecommerce platforms, we selected Shopify as the foundation for the new store. This allowed us to create a custom shopping experience while giving Mr. Ellie Pooh a modern inventory management system for both retail and wholesale customers.

The redesign centered around two connected goals: increasing sales through a modern ecommerce experience and better showcasing the products, artisans, and fair trade mission behind them.

## Results

Mr. Ellie Pooh reported a **10x** increase in sales after launch. Shopify made it easier to manage inventory and purchase products online. New product and artisan photography increased trust in the products while better showcasing their quality and craftsmanship, helping create demand for the artisans who make them.

## Looking back

Mr. Ellie Pooh was my second international project and one that went far beyond a simple website redesign. I'm grateful for the opportunity to work with the company's founder, Dr. Karl. Through our conversations and time together at fair trade conferences, I gained a deeper understanding of fair trade principles and sustainability, along with a greater appreciation for the people behind the products and the impact the Fair Trade Federation has on communities around the world.

Traveling to Sri Lanka and meeting the artisans behind the products while experiencing the manufacturing process firsthand transformed me both personally and professionally.
